How It Works

Getting seen is simpler than you think.

Enroll

Choose your membership plan and join the Priority Care MD network. It takes minutes.

Describe your concern

Browse roughly 150 common physical complaints and get matched to participating physicians who treat your condition.

Submit your request

Select your preferred physician and submit. Priority Care MD reviews it and reaches out to their office on your behalf.

Be seen

Appointment coordinated within 72 hours for new patients, or within 24 hours for established patients. Your insurance handles the visit exactly as it always has.
