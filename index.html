How It Works

From concern to appointment, without the wait.

Five simple steps — the same journey every member takes from the moment something comes up. Scroll to walk through it.

  • Enroll
  • Describe your concern
  • Submit your request
  • Be seen
  • Stay connected
  1. 1

    Enroll

    Choose the membership plan that fits your needs and join the Priority Care MD network. Enrollment takes minutes.

  2. 2

    Describe your concern

    Log into the portal and browse roughly 150 of the most common conditions. Select what applies and see participating physicians who treat exactly that.

  3. 3

    Submit your request

    Select your preferred physician and submit. Priority Care MD confirms the fit, then coordinates with their office on your behalf.

  4. 4

    Be seen

    Typically within 72 hours for priority requests, and within 24 hours for same-day acute needs. Your insurance handles the visit exactly as it always has.

  5. 5

    Stay connected

    After your visit, Priority Care MD follows up through the portal to make sure your appointment was provided and your needs were met.
